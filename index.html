HTML5 is a markup language for structuring and displaying content from the World Wide Web, a core technology of the Internet. HTML5 is the fifth revision of HTML (which was first created in 1990 and its fourth version, HTML4, in 1997) and as of June 2011 is still in development. The main goal of HTML5 development is to improve HTML technology so that it supports the latest multimedia technology, easy to read by humans and also easy to understand by machines.
